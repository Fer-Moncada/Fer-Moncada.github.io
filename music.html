My favorite Rock Band Music

Later searching for similar bands in internet I found my passion. Motörhead is definitely my favorite band, unfortunately I was never able to see them perform live. I like many branches of rock music. Melodic death metal, Rock N Roll and Heavy Metal are my favorites.
